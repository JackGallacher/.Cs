Awfully Big's Advanture. 

Play as a robot giant racing to the garden centre to save his plant from rot!

Instructions:

Get through the level as fast as possible and reach the garden centre.

Destruction of buildings causes your plant to loose life. If health = 0 the game is over and your plant has died. 


Boosts: 

Red Bottles = speed buff. 
Blue Bottles = speed debuff. 
Green bottles = extra life.
